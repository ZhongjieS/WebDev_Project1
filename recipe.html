High-Protein Chinese Recipes

Here are 3 high-protien recipe I enjoying cooking for myself for you to explore if you also want to cook some high protien meals.

1. Sticky Chicken; Broccoli Stir-Fry

Flavorful stir-fry chicken that’s high in protein and ready in ~20 minutes.

Ingredients:

  • Chicken breast or thigh (sliced)
  • Broccoli florets
  • Garlic, ginger
  • Soy sauce, oyster sauce, honey
  • Cornstarch (optional, for thicker sauce)
  • Rice to serve

Instructions:

  1. Stir-fry chicken in a little oil until mostly cooked.
  2. Add broccoli and cook until tender.
  3. Mix soy sauce, oyster sauce, a splash of water, and a little honey in a bowl.
  4. Pour sauce over chicken and broccoli and cook until everything is glossy and coated.
  5. Serve over steamed rice.

2. High-Protein Beijing Style Beef

Crispy strips steak in a sweet-savory Chinese glaze served over rice

Ingredients:

  • Lean steak (sliced thin)
  • Soy sauce, hoisin sauce, sriracha
  • Garlic, onion, bell peppers
  • Cornstarch (light coating for crispiness)

Instructions:

  1. Marinate steak strips with a little soy sauce and cornstarch.
  2. Add light oil and stir-fry until browned and slightly crispy.
  3. Add garlic, onion, bell peppers and cook a couple minutes.
  4. Mix soy sauce, hoisin, sriracha, and water in a bowl.
  5. Pour sauce into the pan and cook until thickened.
  6. Serve hot over a bowl of rice.

3. Mapo Tofu (My favorite)

A protein-packed with tofu and ground pork (or beef). Tofu + meat gives great texture and protein.

Ingredients:

  • Firm or silken tofu (cubed)
  • Ground pork or beef
  • Garlic, ginger
  • Doubanjiang (spicy bean paste) or chili sauce
  • Soy sauce + chicken broth or water
  • Green onions to finish

Instructions:

  1. Add light oil and brown the ground meat with garlic and ginger.
  2. Add doubanjiang and cook briefly until fragrant.
  3. Gently add tofu cubes and broth/soy sauce.
  4. Simmer for ~5–10 min so tofu absorbs flavor.
  5. Garnish with green onions and serve with rice.
  • Bold flavor, hearty meal
  • Great source of plant and animal protein
  • Easiest to make in my opinion
