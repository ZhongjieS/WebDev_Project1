1. Sticky Chicken; Broccoli Stir-Fry

Flavorful stir-fry chicken thatâ€™s high in protein and ready in ~20 minutes.

Ingredients:

  • Chicken breast or thigh (sliced)
  • Broccoli florets
  • Garlic, ginger
  • Soy sauce, oyster sauce, honey
  • Cornstarch (optional, for thicker sauce)
  • Rice to serve

Instructions:

  1. Stir-fry chicken in a little oil until mostly cooked.
  2. Add broccoli and cook until tender.
  3. Mix soy sauce, oyster sauce, a splash of water, and a little honey in a bowl.
  4. Pour sauce over chicken and broccoli and cook until everything is glossy and coated.
  5. Serve over steamed rice.
